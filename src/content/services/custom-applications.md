## What I build

I design and build web applications, internal tools, and A.I.-integrated systems from scratch—or extend existing ones that have stalled. Every project starts with a free scoping consultation where we define requirements, timeline, and deliverables before a single line of code is written.

- **Full-stack web applications**—Marketing sites, dashboards, portals, and interactive tools built with modern frameworks that ship fast and hold up at scale.
- **A.I./ML integration**—Embed LLM capabilities (conversational interfaces, content generation, classification, extraction) directly into your product, not as a bolted-on chatbot but as a native feature.
- **Internal tools**—Admin dashboards, data pipelines, reporting systems, and workflow management tools that your team actually wants to use.
- **Design systems**—Component libraries, token systems, and brand guidelines that keep your product visually coherent as it scales.
