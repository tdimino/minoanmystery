Hi! I'm Tom di Mino. I live in Rosendale, NY, where I write, design, & consult in all things A.I.

If Walt Whitman “contained multitudes,” then I'm as 'cross-functional' as they come. Born a writer and voracious reader of science-fiction novels, I transitioned into tech at an early age, christened on message boards, MMORPGs, and historic turn-based games.

After rebranding a cloud-based SaaS suite for higher-ed, I sharpened into a copywriter with SEO acumen, before leading content strategy for enterprise-grade clients like Dolby Labs, and the American College of Surgeons.

In the last 3 years, I've co-led projects at Google, and Chan Zuckerberg Initiative, designing and launching learning curricula geared toward ML scientists, A.I. developers, and open-source in general. Recently, I steered the product design and 'agentic' flow behind an A.I./LLM "meeting assistant" for wealth advisors at the largest bank in the world.

As a life-long language lover, and die-hard nerd at heart, I verse myself in the many uses and limitations of LLMs from OpenAI, Anthropic, and Meta, and brainstorm with the brightest minds behind the most prescient frameworks in 'agentic' A.I. today.

When I’m not consulting for J.P. Morgan through my LLC, or delving deeper into TypeScript and LLM-assisted coding, you'll find me hiking with my partner in upstate New York, performing live poetry, and imbuing the digital with soul.

My industry experience

As a writer, designer, and AI programmer, I bring a syncretic and empathetic lens to any team or organization.
