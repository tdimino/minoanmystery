## CampusCruiser (2016-2017)

### Role: Marketing Specialist

CampusCruiser was a startup and developer of the first cloud email and intranet/LMS for 100+ US colleges and universities, partnered with Ellucian.

### Achievements
- Rebranded SaaS portal "Strata" and LMS "Knosys"
- Closed the first new sales in over 4 years
- Redesigned and built flagship product demos
- Applied Material Design principles, mobile responsiveness, and gamification
- Attended industry trade shows and presented demos
- Initiated business partnership with William Paterson game design team
- Co-led focus group at Widener University

### Impact
The rebrand directly enabled new sales after a 4-year drought. This early experience in SaaS marketing informed Tom's understanding of product positioning and user experience.

## 1SEO (2017-2018)

### Role: I.T. Support & Digital Marketing Content Writer

1SEO is a B2B/B2C digital marketing agency dominating paid and organic search throughout the Delaware River Valley and Philadelphia areas.

### Achievements
- Composed PRs and blog posts for dozens of clients weekly
- Specialized in diverse industries: biotech, legal, medical, manufacturing, dentistry, real estate, HVAC, IT
- Joined kick-off calls to identify voice, tone, and target audiences
- Collaborated within teams of content editors, writers, and SEO strategists
- Wrote landing page copy, eCommerce product pages, and case studies

### Impact
This high-volume, multi-industry experience developed rapid writing skills and adaptability across domains—foundational for later enterprise work.

## Renaissance (2018-2019)

Renaissance is a Philadelphia-based digital marketing agency offering its own CMS-POS-CRM and eCommerce platform.

### Achievements
- Planned, wrote, and edited landing page copy consistently ranking on 1st page of Google
- Managed SEO for a dozen eCommerce sites using Google Analytics and SEMRush
- Generated more than $2 million in eCommerce revenue in less than a year

The $2M+ revenue achievement demonstrated that content strategy could drive measurable business outcomes—a principle that carries through all of Tom's work.
